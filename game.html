Franks and Buns - Free Hot Dog Arcade Game

Franks and Buns is a free browser-based arcade game. Tap to launch your hot dog upward and land it in buns as they scroll past. Dodge the chef fork obstacle, collect condiment bonuses for score multipliers, and beat your high score.

Features include mustard, ketchup, relish, cheese and jalapeno condiment bonuses worth 2x to 10x points, a streak multiplier system, box completion bonuses, and bonus dogs worth 1000 points. The game gets faster and more challenging as you progress.

Play free with no download required at franksandbuns.com. Works on iPhone, Android, and desktop browsers.
