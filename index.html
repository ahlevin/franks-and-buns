Franks & Buns — Free Hot Dog Arcade Game

Franks & Buns is a free, mobile-friendly arcade game inspired by Flappy Bird. Guide your flying hot dog (the frank) through a scrolling kitchen and land it perfectly into buns at different heights — on the counter, inside ovens, on cabinet shelves, and even in a dog's mouth!

How to Play

Tap or click to boost your frank upward. Release to let gravity pull it down. Time your drops to land inside the buns as they scroll past. Miss a bun and you lose one of your three lives. Land franks back-to-back to build a streak and multiply your score.

Game Features

  • One-tap controls — easy to learn, hard to master
  • Condiment buns (mustard, ketchup, relish, cheese, jalapeño) for bonus points
  • Box system — land 8 franks to complete a box and earn a big bonus
  • Streak multipliers — ON A ROLL, FRANKS ON FIRE, WIENER WIZARD
  • Increasing speed and difficulty as you pack more boxes
  • Local high score saved in your browser
  • Works on iPhone, Android, and desktop browsers — no download required

Scoring

Each frank landed in a regular bun earns 100 points. Condiment buns multiply your score — mustard is 2x, ketchup 3x, relish and cheese 5x, and the rare jalapeño bun is worth 10x. Complete a box of 8 franks for a box bonus equal to the box number times 100. Chain together consecutive landings to activate streak multipliers of up to 3x.
