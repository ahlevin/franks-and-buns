How to Play

👆

Tap to Boost

Tap or click to launch your frank upward. Release to let gravity do its thing.

🥖

Land in the Bun

Time your drop perfectly to land in the scrolling hot dog rolls. Different locations score different points.

🍴

Dodge the Fork

The chef's fork rises between buns without warning. Hit it and lose a life. You only get three.

🏆

Beat Your Best

Chain landings for streak multipliers, grab condiment bonuses, and fill boxes for big point bursts.

What's Inside

🟡

Condiment Bonuses

Mustard, ketchup, relish, cheese, jalapeño — each squeeze bottle multiplies your score.

📦

Box System

Land 8 franks to complete a box, earn a big bonus, and watch the kitchen speed up.

🔥

Streak Multipliers

Hit 3, 5, or 10 in a row to unlock On A Roll, Franks On Fire, and Wiener Wizard modes.

🐕

Dog Bun Event

A dog trots through with a bun in its mouth. Land in it for a massive bonus score.

🍴

Chef's Fork

The chef's arm rises between buns without warning. Dodge the tines or lose a life.

📱

Play Anywhere

Mobile-first design. Tap on phone, click on desktop. No app install required.
