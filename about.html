About Franks & Buns 🌭

Franks & Buns is a one-tap arcade game where timing and precision are everything. A sneaky dog stole a hot dog and made a run for it. When the chef gives chase and runs into the pup, the wiener goes flying. That airborne hot dog is you. Guide it into buns, dodge the chef's fork, and rack up the highest score possible.

Meet the Characters

🌭

The Frank

That's you. A plucky little sausage with grill marks, just trying to find a good bun.

The Chef

He wants his hot dog back. His fork shoots up without warning. Dodge it or lose a life.

The Dog

The chaos agent who started it all. After box 3 the dog appears in gameplay worth 1,000 points.

How to Play

Tap, click, or press Space to boost your frank upward. Release to let gravity bring it down. Time your drops to land in the open bun slots as they scroll past from right to left. Buns appear at different heights on counters, shelves, ovens, and fridges. Miss a bun, hit the floor, or collide with the chef's fork and you lose a life. Three lives and it's game over.

Scoring & Multipliers

Every frank you land scores 100 base points. The real fun is in the multipliers. Condiment buns marked with squeeze bottles multiply your score and landing in a grilled bun after box 6 earns a flat 1,000 point bonus.

Build streaks for additional multipliers land 3 in a row for On A Roll (1.5x), 5 in a row for Franks On Fire (2x), and 10 in a row for the legendary Wiener Wizard mode (3x). Miss once and your streak resets.

The Box System

Every 6 franks you land fills a box. Complete a box for a point bonus and a speed increase. The further you get, the faster and more chaotic the kitchen becomes. Grilled buns start appearing after box 6 land in one for a massive 1,000 point bonus and the satisfying "LET THEM COOK!" callout.

Bonus Rounds

Dog Parade Bonus Round

Complete 4 boxes and the kitchen goes wild. Dogs stream across the screen for 5 seconds tap to land your frank on each one for 500 points each. After the parade ends you earn an extra life. The bonus round repeats every 4 boxes.

The Wienerboard

Think you've got what it takes? The Wienerboard tracks the top 5 scores of all time. Beat a top 5 score and you'll be prompted to enter your nickname. Can you claim the #1 spot?
