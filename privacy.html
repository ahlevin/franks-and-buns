Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Franks & Buns ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit franksandbuns.com (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play the game. However, the following data may be collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, browser type, and device type, collected by Google Analytics and AdSense.
  • Cookies — small text files placed on your device by Google AdSense and Analytics to enable advertising and measure site performance.
  • Local storage — we store your high score locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • IP address — may be collected by Google's advertising infrastructure to serve relevant ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To measure and improve site performance
  • To remember your high score between sessions (stored locally only)

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this site. Google may use cookies and web beacons to collect data to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data, see How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

4. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function.
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics, used to understand how visitors use the site.
  • Advertising cookies — Google AdSense, used to serve relevant advertisements.

Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland will see a consent prompt before any non-essential cookies are set. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your cookies and declining on your next visit.

5. Your Rights (EEA / UK / Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under GDPR and applicable law:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to rectification or erasure
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

Since we store only anonymous analytics data and high scores remain on your own device, there is no personal data to request or delete from our systems. For advertising data held by Google, please contact Google directly.

6. Children's Privacy

This site is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
